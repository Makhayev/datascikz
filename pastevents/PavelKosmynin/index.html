Guest talk about differences in Data Science positions with Pavel Kosmynin

Greetings, everyone! DataSci team is pleased to invite you to the guest talk with Pavel Kosmynin about different data science positions. Data science is a broad term that defines many different branches: machine learning engineer, data analyst, data engineer, etc. If you want to become a data scientist, you have to know what type of data science job better suits you, as the difference in their duties is sometimes critical. PavelKosmynin Kosmynin, Data Science team lead at BI Innovations, has years of experience in data science, and will unravel and explain every type of data science position.
